Hotel Details

Set in a high-rise building in a business district, this sophisticated apartment hotel is 9 km from Table Mountain National Park and 2 km from V&A Waterfront. It's 850 m from Cape Town train station.

Sleek apartments include free Wi-Fi, flat-screens, air-conditioning and kitchens, plus seating & dining areas. Some have washers and/or balconies. Upscale penthouse units add furnished balconies with views of the city and surrounding nature.

Parking is free. There's a retail area with a coffee shop, a business centre, and restaurants. An outdoor pool features a lawn and a deck with sunloungers. There's also a rooftop sundeck.
